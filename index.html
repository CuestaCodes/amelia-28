GROWTH
By RJC

It felt like we hadn't spoken in years
In fact, we hadn't even even met

Spooled with our own memories
Favorable conditions on the set

Swollen by eachother's warm embrace
Deep surfaces were split in two

Neither cookbook nor map handed out
Served adventures on the road as food

Seeking nourishment and seldom bored
We yearn to gift something true

From here on, I will promise that;
If you water me, I will water you
